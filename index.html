#LivinginLulu

This is a social media marketing campaign done for Lululemon. The big idea behind the campaign is #LivinginLulu. This is a campaign targeted to young adult women that is connected to the release of a new subscription service Lululemon offers. The story behind #LivinginLulu is the idea of wearing Lululemon wherever you are; practically living in it. Whether you are shopping, going to school, working out, or just chilling at home, Lululemon can be worn whenever and wherever.

Graduate Hotels

This project for Graduate Hotels focuses on brand expansion by offering a new experience targeting the parents of graduating students. This experience would include discounts, rewards, etc. The experience is made up of “Parent Packages'' that center around Fall and Spring Graduation, with smaller experiences available around orientation weekends. Graduation weekend is a crowded mess and our proposal gives parents the opportunity to celebrate their children from the comfort of Graduate Hotel grounds. The Graduate brand fits well with the Parent Package proposal as Graduate is a unique and niche company well known for its authentic vibe.
